About Me

    Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at the ECE department of National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Haizhou Li and Prof. Luis Fernando D'Haro. I am also an NUS-Robert Bosch (SEA) IPP student and Dr. Thomas Friedrichs is my supervisor in Robert Bosch (SEA). My research interests include Automatic Dialogue Evaluation, Dialogue Systems and Natural Language Processing in general. Previously, I obtained my double degree in Bussiness and Computer Engineering from Nanyang Technological University.
